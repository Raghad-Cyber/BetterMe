Help & FAQ

Answers to common questions and tips to get the most out of BetterMe.

How do I create my first habit?

Go to the habit setup page, enter a name, choose 30/60/90 days, select daily or weekly tracking, and tap Save.

Where can I see all my habits?

Open the dashboard for a quick overview. Each habit has a card with progress, streak, and a quick Check in button.

How do streaks work?

Streaks increase when you log consecutive days (or weeks) without missing. Missing a day (or week) resets the streak.

Can I edit or delete a habit?

Yes. Open the habit’s details page and use Edit Habit or Delete Habit in the Danger Zone section.

Daily vs. weekly tracking—what should I choose?

Choose daily for small, frequent actions. Choose weekly for goals completed once per week.

Do I get reminders?

In this prototype, reminders are just a toggle. You can add real notifications later with JavaScript and notifications APIs.

My progress isn’t saving—why?

This static prototype doesn’t store data yet. You can add persistence with localStorage or a backend later.

How It Works

  1. Create a habit in the setup page.
  2. Track it from the dashboard with quick check-ins.
  3. Open the habit details to see streaks and visual progress.
